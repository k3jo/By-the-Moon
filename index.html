Farmer's Almanac

Lunar Rhythms

The moon controls ocean tides, influences the groundwater tables beneath our feet and the movement of fluids in plants. Even continental land masses are said to rise 2 to 3 feet in elevation with the passage of the moon. Understanding the effects, and timing your gardening chores accordingly, is the basis of moon gardening.

Moon gardening follows the Farmer's Almanac tradition where the cycles of the moon affect plant growth. On entering a date, this app provides information on the name of the moon at each month, what point in age the moon is at, the stage, and if the light of the moon is increasing or decreasing. It also provides some information on the status of moon in addition to what actions to take in gardening, if one were to follow the Farmer's almanac and the moon
